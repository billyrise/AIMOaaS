1. One-page summary (for audit firms)

What it is

Evidence Pack generator plus managed operations. AIMOaaS produces audit-ready deliverables (AI-BOM, Change Ledger, Evidence Bundle) and can support ongoing monitoring.

Why now

In many engagements, the bottleneck is reconstructing who used what, when, and with which data from scattered logs. Clients need structured, non-repudiable evidence; assurance teams need to focus on judgment, not evidence chasing.

What changes

Less evidence chasing, more professional judgment. Your team gets a consistent Evidence Pack aligned with AIMO Standard; you retain assurance conclusions and client advisory.

2. Joint offering templates

Choose the model that fits your firm. Below: who does what (RACI-style).

Referral model

  • Audit firm: Introduces client; owns relationship and assurance conclusion.
  • AIMOaaS: Delivers Evidence Pack and optional ops; no direct client branding.
  • Commercial: Referral fee or revenue share as agreed.

Co-branded offering

  • Audit firm: Leads sales and delivery; positions as joint offering; signs client.
  • AIMOaaS: Provides Evidence Pack production, validator, and delivery playbook; supports under your brand.
  • Commercial: Co-branded pricing; revenue share or fixed fee per engagement.

White-label delivery

  • Audit firm: Owns client contract, branding, and assurance; AIMOaaS invisible to end client.
  • AIMOaaS: Supplies Evidence Pack and processes; you deliver as your own service.
  • Commercial: White-label fee; volume or term agreements as needed.

3. Delivery playbook (2–4 weeks)

Data intake checklist

Minimum inputs (examples):

  • Network / proxy logs (e.g. outbound HTTP/HTTPS)
  • Security / EDR logs (if available)
  • Basic policy or governance docs (optional)

Output list

  • AI-BOM (AI bill of materials)
  • Change Ledger
  • Evidence Bundle (manifest, hashes, signatures; AIMO Standard)
  • Findings and remediation plan (gaps and recommendations)

QA steps

  • Validator run on Evidence Bundle (integrity check)
  • Consistency check between AI-BOM, Change Ledger, and Bundle
  • Send-back process: defined handoff and revision cycle for corrections

4. Security & data handling

Principles we follow (specifics are agreed per engagement):

  • Customer-controlled storage: Where possible, evidence and logs remain in storage controlled by the customer or the audit firm.
  • Access control and retention: Aligned with your and your client’s policies (access, retention, deletion).
  • Execution options: Optional local execution or cloud execution—we can align with your planned deployment and compliance requirements.
