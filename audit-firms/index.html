AIMOaaS for Audit Firms

Evidence chaos and re-requests erode margins. We deliver Evidence Packs you can attach to working papers—with a clear responsibility boundary: AIMO = Proof, your firm = Assurance.

Responsibility boundary

AIMOaaS: Evidence generation and integrity checks (Proof). Your firm: Assurance conclusion and client advisory. We do not opine on control effectiveness or issue assurance reports; you retain judgment and client relationship.

Where it hurts

In AI governance engagements, evidence collection, formatting, and reconciliation dominate effort. Re-requests and non-repudiation gaps extend cycles. Many firms are adding AI to assurance offerings; consistent, attachable evidence helps you spend time on judgment—not evidence chasing.

What AIMOaaS Provides

  • Evidence Pack — A complete set of deliverables ready for audit working papers (AI-BOM, Change Ledger, minimum evidence checklist, gaps and remediation plan).
  • Evidence Bundle — Manifest, hashes, and signatures (AIMO Standard Evidence Bundle) with a Validator for verification and non-repudiation.
  • Continuous evidence — Ongoing monitoring and evidence generation for sustained assurance (future extension).
