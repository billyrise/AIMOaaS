Case Studies: Evidence Pack in Practice

From scattered evidence to audit-ready Evidence Pack

Situation

Shadow AI visibility was low; evidence was scattered across tools and teams. The client (and their auditors) faced repeated re-requests and long audit prep cycles because deliverables were not standardized or integrity-checked.

Action

We delivered an Evidence Pack: AI-BOM, Change Ledger, and Evidence Bundle (manifest, hashes, signatures) aligned with AIMO Standard. Data intake used network/security logs and basic policy docs; human review and validator runs were part of the workflow.

Outcome (target KPIs)

  • Evidence readiness improved: one coherent pack attachable to working papers.
  • Audit prep time reduced (target: e.g. 30–50% vs. pre-pack baseline).
  • Re-request rate lowered (target: fewer follow-up rounds; integrity checks and repeatable workflow reduce back-and-forth).

What auditors care about

  • Integrity checks — Evidence Bundle with hashes/signatures and validator; non-repudiation support.
  • Repeatable workflow — Same structure every time; easier to scope and price for your firm.
  • Responsibility boundary — AIMO = proof generation; audit firm = assurance conclusion and client relationship.
