Case Studies: How Organizations Implement AI Governance

Anonymous examples of how we help companies with Shadow AI discovery, governance build, and managed operations. All stories are based on real engagements; identities and details are anonymized.

Manufacturing

From no visibility to governed AI use

Challenge

A global manufacturing company had no single view of AI use across sites. Engineering and operations teams used various AI tools without central approval; compliance and IT could not assess risk or cost.

Approach

We started with a Tier 1 Shadow AI audit to map existing use and hotspots. Then we designed an AI governance framework (Tier 2): allowlist, request flow, and a digital register. The client chose to keep operations in-house with our playbooks; Tier 3 monitoring was optional for later.

Outcome

Full visibility of AI tools in use, a clear policy and approval process, and evidence ready for internal audit. The organization is now in a position to add 24/365 monitoring when they scale further.
