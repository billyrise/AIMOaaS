AI Governance: A Complete Guide

This guide covers why AI governance matters, key regulations (EU AI Act, ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF), Shadow AI risk, and how managed operations help you stay compliant without blocking innovation.

Why AI governance matters

Unmanaged AI use leads to data leakage, duplicate spend, and regulatory exposure. Governance is not only about prohibition—it is about enabling safe, traceable use aligned with your risk appetite and with international standards.

Key regulations and standards

EU AI Act, ISO/IEC 42001 (AI management system), and NIST AI RMF provide overlapping requirements. AIMO Standard unifies these so you can prepare one coherent set of controls and evidence. See also our EU AI Act page and Glossary.

Shadow AI and visibility

Shadow AI is unsanctioned use of AI tools by employees. Discovery via logs and CASB, then governance design and 24/365 monitoring, is the path AIMOaaS™ supports. Details: Shadow AI.

Next steps
