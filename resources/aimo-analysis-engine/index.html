AIMO Analysis Engine: How It Works

AIMO Analysis Engine is the technology behind AIMOaaS™: it scans logs, identifies AI use and risk, and feeds into expert-led operations. This page explains the flow and how it differs from general-purpose AI agents.

Log scanning and risk detection

We use CASB and network logs to identify AI and GenAI usage. The engine identifies who uses what, where data may be going, and anomalies (e.g. confidential uploads, unusual API spend). Results are reviewed by analysts in a Human-in-the-Loop process.

Governance-first design

Unlike general-purpose agents built for productivity or coding, AIMO Analysis Engine is built for governance: visibility, policy alignment, incident response, and audit-ready evidence. It supports AIMO Standard, EU AI Act, ISO 42001, and NIST AI RMF.

Expert operations

Detection is only the first step. Our team runs 24/365 monitoring, interprets findings, and takes action (e.g. blocking, escalation) with clear accountability. Contact us to learn more.
