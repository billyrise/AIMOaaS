EU AI Act: Compliance & Fines

The EU AI Act classifies AI systems by risk. Non-compliance can lead to fines of up to 7% of global annual revenue. AIMO Standard aligns with its requirements to help you prepare evidence and controls.

Risk tiers and obligations

High-risk AI systems face strict documentation (e.g. Annex IV), conformity assessments, and post-market monitoring. Prohibited AI practices are banned outright. AIMOaaS™ supports visibility and governance so you can map your use cases to the Act and maintain audit-ready evidence.

How AIMO Standard helps

AIMO Standard unifies EU AI Act, ISO 42001, and NIST AI RMF in one framework. Our managed operations help you track AI use and prepare the documentation and controls regulators expect.
