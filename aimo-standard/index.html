What is AIMO Standard?

AIMO Standard is a human-readable AI governance specification that addresses enterprise risk from Shadow AI and agentic AI with broad tool privileges. It defines a shared taxonomy, minimum evidence requirements, and a Validator for consistency—improving audit explainability and remediation.

Taxonomy

8 dimensions, 91 codes for consistent AI use classification

Minimum Evidence

MUST-level checklist for evidence readiness

Validator

Structural and consistency checks

Coverage Map

Mapping to major frameworks

AIMO Standard’s Coverage Map provides traceability between the specification and major regulations and standards. One evidence base can support explainability across multiple regimes. It does not provide legal advice or guarantee compliance.

EU AI Act

EU-wide AI regulation. Mapping to high-level documentation and record-keeping requirements.

ISO/IEC 42001

AI management system. Mapping of standard themes to AIMO elements.

NIST AI RMF

Govern, Map, Measure, Manage. Mapping to AIMO evidence and artifacts.

ISMS

ISO/IEC 27001/27002 themes: change management, access control, logging, evidence integrity.
